Our Commitment to You

Located in Lomira, Wisconsin, Schaefer Auto is your trusted destination for reliable used vehicles at unbeatable prices. We take pride in offering a diverse selection of makes, models, years, and mileage options, ensuring you’ll find a vehicle that fits your lifestyle and budget. Don’t see exactly what you’re looking for? No problem—our team will go the extra mile to help you find the perfect match.

We know buying a vehicle can feel overwhelming, which is why we focus on creating a stress-free experience. Visit Schaefer Auto for your next purchase, where we’ll work with you to find a car tailored to your needs, all while ensuring a quick and hassle-free process. Proudly serving the local community and beyond since 2021, we’re here to help you drive away with confidence.

Meet the Team

We are an experienced team looking to help you!

Caleb Schaefer

Buyer

Schaefer Auto originated from Caleb’s passion for “flipping” vehicles, ATV’s, boats, trailers, etc. He has continued to grow the business on his own, while remaining hands on. Caleb works to ensure the vehicle inventory is always abundant and diverse, provides communication with customers during their vehicle search, and facilitates vehicle sales.

Roman

Auto Technician

Roman was the first addition to Schaefer Auto. He joined us with education from the Universal Technical Institute in automotive technician training. Prior to joining our team, he had multiple years of experience working for a local dealership and service shop, where he focused primarily on GM and Ford vehicles.

Randy

Auto Technician

Randy has immense experience in the automotive industry. He is an important part of the team, applying his almost 30 years, as both an auto technician and in a management role.

Holly
