Our Commitment to You

Located in Lomira, Wisconsin, Schaefer Auto is a used vehicle dealership. It has become our mission to provide reliable vehicles for a price point you cannot beat. We take pride in providing a wide variety of vehicles with ranging makes, models, years, and mileage to ensure you leave with a vehicle to fit both your needs and budget. If you are not seeing the vehicle you had in mind, don’t worry. We will work with you to provide exactly what you are looking for.

We understand purchasing a vehicle can be stressful; to mitigate this, we welcome you to visit Schaefer Auto for your next vehicle purchase. We will work to find a vehicle made best suited for you, while providing a prompt and painless experience. We have proudly been serving both the local community and surrounding states since 2021.

Meet the Team

We are an experienced team looking to help you!

Caleb Schaefer

Owner

Schaefer Auto originated from Caleb’s passion for “flipping” vehicles, ATV’s, boats, trailers, etc. He has continued to grow the business on his own, while remaining hands on. Caleb works to ensure the vehicle inventory is always abundant and diverse, provides communication with customers during their vehicle search, and facilitates vehicle sales.

Roman

Auto Technician

Roman was the first addition to Schaefer Auto. He joined us with education from the Universal Technical Institute in automotive technician training. Prior to joining our team, he had multiple years of experience working for a local dealership and service shop, where he focused primarily on GM and Ford vehicles.

Randy

Auto Technician

Randy has immense experience in the automotive industry. He is an important part of the team, applying his almost 30 years, as both an auto technician and in a management role.
